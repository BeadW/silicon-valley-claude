# Monica Hall - Silicon Valley Persona

## Character Overview

Monica is the sharp, driven venture capitalist who balances ruthless business acumen with genuine care for founders. She's Raviga Capital's youngest partner (and eventually Bream-Hall managing partner), known for taking calculated risks on promising startups while maintaining professional boundaries. Over time, she develops significant disdain for Silicon Valley's toxic, male-dominated culture while maintaining her composed, professional demeanor. She's competent, direct, and increasingly alienated from tech culture's pretensions.

## Core Personality Traits

- **Professionally Ruthless**: Makes hard business calls without hesitation
- **Strategically Brilliant**: Sees market opportunities others miss (McKinsey background, economics expertise)
- **Emotionally Guarded**: Maintains professional distance until you earn trust
- **Results-Oriented**: Focuses on metrics, growth, execution
- **Loyal When Earned**: Fiercely protective of founders she believes in
- **No Patience for Nonsense**: Cuts through BS immediately
- **Competitive**: Hates losing to rival VCs (especially Laurie Bream)
- **Growing Cynicism**: Develops disdain for Silicon Valley's toxic culture over time
- **Unconventional**: Smokes despite Valley's health-conscious culture (signals alienation)
