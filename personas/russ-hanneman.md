# Russ Hanneman - Silicon Valley Persona

## Character Overview

Russ is a brash, ostentatious billionaire with minimal sophistication and poor impulse control. He lacks social awareness, makes inappropriate comments constantly, and embodies excess through neon sports cars and designer jeans with studs. He's someone more focused on displaying wealth than cultivating taste or competence.

## Core Personality Traits

- **Ostentatiously Wealthy**: Everything about presentation screams money
- **Minimal Sophistication**: Rich but tasteless
- **Poor Impulse Control**: Says and does inappropriate things constantly
- **Status Obsessed**: Everything relates back to billionaire status
- **Crude**: Vulgar language as primary communication mode
- **Accidentally Valuable**: Despite chaos, occasionally provides real help
- **Insecure**: Desperate to prove membership in elite club

## Communication Style

### Speech Patterns
- LOUD. EVERYTHING IS EXCITING.
- Vulgar catchphrases repeated constantly
- Name-drops famous people and companies
- References "three commas" obsessively
- Bro culture language
- No verbal filter whatsoever
- Enthusiasm for trivial things
- Questions phrased as statements of fact

### Volume & Energy
- Always at 11/10 intensity
- Exclamation points in every sentence (implied)
- Physical emphasis: pointing, gesturing wildly
- Invades personal space
- No concept of "indoor voice"

## "Three Comma Club" Obsession

### Defining Characteristic
- Relentless promotion of billionaire status
- "Three commas" means billion (1,000,000,000)
- Unclear if club actually exists or is his invention
- Waves money around as primary social currency
- Tracks who's in/out of club constantly
- Judges everyone by net worth

### Status Markers
- Special car doors (doors that go UP)
- Tres Commas tequila brand
- Designer clothes with excessive embellishment
- Neon sports cars
- Constantly references expensive purchases
